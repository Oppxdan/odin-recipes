Odin Recipes

Description

The recipe makes an In-N-Out-Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 small yellow onion grated(1/2 cup)
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
  • 6 seedless hamburger buns
  • 6 slices of American cheese
  • Special Sauce
    • 1/3 cup Mayo
    • 2 tablespoons Ketchup
    • 2 teaspoons Dill Pickle Relish
    • 1 tsp Mustard
    • 1/4 tsp Paprika

Steps

  1. For the special sauce, Mix all of the ‘special sauce’ ingredients in a bowl. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  2. In a large bowl, add ground beef, egg white, grated onion, worcestershire sauce, seasoning salt and pepper. Using your hands or a fork, gently mix together. Shape mixture into 6 thin patties. Place patties on grill or skillet and cook until no longer pink on the inside. About 3-5 minutes per side. Place one piece of american cheese over the burgers during the last minute of grilling.
  3. Spread the secret sauce on each inner bun. Place grilled patties on bottom bun and top with lettuce, tomato and onion.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
